Sniper:



Country of Origin: Australia
Role: Support

“In his former life as a tracker of dangerous game in the unforgiving Australian outback, the Sniper would spend months by himself. Prolonged isolation taught him a valuable lesson: You don't have to rely on other people if you never miss.” - Valve


Weapons:


Sniper Rifle:

Slow rate of fire but deadly to most classes, try aiming for the head to deal massive damage.

Submachine Gun:

When you’re the one trapped in a corner try using this semi-automatic. Switch between the sniper rifle and the s.m.g. for far and short range enemies.

Kukri:

An exotic curved blade make sure to switch to this weapon when you hear a spy creeping behind you.
